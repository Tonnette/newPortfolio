FILM BIO

Tonnette is Sydney based a trained writer and director. She is a graduate of the Australian Film Television and Radio School (AFTRS), the Victorian College of the Arts (VCA) and the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). Her films have won numerous awards and have screened at over 200 festivals internationally and nationally, including Oscar recognised festivals. Her web-series, Love Bytes has had over 9 million youtube views, and the follow up series, Romp, has won numerous international awards. Her feature documentary, Holding Hands is a multi-award winning film that is distributed by CFMDC Distribution in Canada.
